How It Works

1

Capture

Position yourself in front of your webcam and perform sign language gestures naturally.

2

Process

Mediapipe Holistic extracts 3D keypoints from your hands, face, and body pose in real-time.

3

Analyze

Our LSTM neural network processes the sequence of keypoints to recognize the sign language gestures.

4

Interpret

The detected gestures are translated into text or speech, enabling seamless communication.
